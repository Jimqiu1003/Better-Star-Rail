# Privacy

## Local processing

Screen capture, OCR, templates, automation state, task files, logs and optional failure screenshots are processed locally.

## No credentials

The application must not request or store game account passwords, cookies, tokens, verification codes or browser sessions.

## Screenshots

Screenshots may contain UID, nickname, chat messages or other personal information. They are not uploaded automatically. Users can disable diagnostic screenshot retention and clear stored data.

## Telemetry

v0.1 has no silent telemetry. Any future crash upload must be opt-in, previewable and separately reviewed.

## User control

Users can view, export and delete settings, logs, recordings and screenshots.
